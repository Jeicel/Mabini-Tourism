Featured Destinations

Explore the most stunning tourist spots in Mabini, Batangas

Popular

Camp Netanya Resort

Experience the magic of Santorini in the Philippines! This stunning resort features Greek-inspired architecture, infinity pools overlooking the ocean, and world-class diving facilities in Anilao's crystal-clear waters.

Best for Diving

Sombrero Island

Named after its distinctive hat-like shape, this pristine island paradise offers some of the best diving in Southeast Asia. Explore vibrant coral gardens, encounter exotic marine life, and relax on powdery white sand beaches.

Luxury

Eagle Point Resort

An exclusive beachfront sanctuary featuring stunning sunset views, infinity pools, and premium diving experiences. Perfect for couples, families, and adventure seekers looking for luxury in a tropical paradise.

About Mabini

Mabini is a first-class municipality in Batangas, Philippines, known as the diving capital of the country. With pristine waters, diverse marine life, and world-class resorts, it's a paradise for adventure seekers and beach lovers.
