MABINI
As the diving capital of the Philippines, Mabini is blessed with world-class resorts, stunning islands, and breathtaking mountain views. Discover the best of Batangas—where adventure meets paradise.
Tourist Spots

Camp Netanya

Experience Santorini in Batangas! Camp Netanya offers stunning architecture, infinity pools, and direct access to the vibrant marine life of Anilao.

Sombrero Island

Named for its hat-like shape, Sombrero Island is a diver’s paradise with crystal-clear waters, coral gardens, and white sand beaches perfect for relaxation.

Eagle Point Beach and Dive Resort

Relax by the pool and enjoy breathtaking sunsets over the ocean. Sunset Resort is perfect for unwinding and capturing beautiful moments in Mabini.
